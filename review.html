Review Process

  • The review process is managed by the Editors-in-Chief (EiCs), Associate Editors (AEs), Editorial Board Members (EBMs) and External Reviewers (ERs). EiCs, AEs and EBMs are listed on the homepage
  • Submissions are by default single-blind with authors indicating their identity in the paper, while reviewers remain anonymous.
  • Upon submission of a manuscript, an EiC can choose to Desk-Reject a paper, or invite an AE to be Editor. Exceptionally, an EiC may choose to invite an EBM to be Editor.
  • The Editor can recommend to the EiC to desk-reject a paper. Otherwise the Editor should find a minimum of three Reviewers for a paper. Reviewers can be EBMs, or can be external to the EB.
  • Reviews are expected within four weeks of acceptance of a review invite, but the Editor can choose to offer Reviewers more time.
  • Upon receiving three reviews that the Editor considers to be of high quality, the Editor should submit a Decision Letter justifying one of four Recommendations: Reject, Major Revision, Minor Revision, Accept.
  • In case that a paper receives two Rejects, the Editor may choose to send a Decision Letter rejecting the paper without receiving a third review.
  • An Editor’s Recommendation can be overturned by an EiC vote (simple majority with the longest-serving EiC having a casting vote).
  • An assigned Editor can be changed via an EiC vote (simple majority with the longest-serving EiC having a casting vote).
  • Resubmissions of Major Revisions are expected within eight weeks of sending a decision letter. Resubmissions of Minor Revisions are expected within four weeks. The Editor can choose to offer authors more time for resubmission.
  • Resubmissions are expected to include a detailed Response Letter replying to Reviewer and Editor comments, indicating and justifying the changes implemented (if any).
  • The Editor will decide which Reviewers to (re)invite for reviewing Resubmissions (if any).
  • Upon resubmission of a Major Revision, if the Editor’s recommendation is a second Major Revision, the manuscript will rather be Rejected (“Two-Strike Rule”).
  • Papers that are rejected cannot be resubmitted within one year of the Rejection notification, independently of changes made to the paper. An EiC can rule when a submission is a resubmission.
